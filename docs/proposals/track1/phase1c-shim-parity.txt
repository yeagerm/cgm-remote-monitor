Known parity gap
================

  TypeError: Cannot read properties of null (reading 'split')
    at C (bundle.app.js:2:1292338)
    at u (bundle.app.js:2:1286922)
    ...
    at Context.<anonymous> (tests/profileeditor.test.js:133:26)
       --> $('#pe_records_add').click();

This is a behavior diff between jsdom 11 (returns "" for some absent
attribute or property) and jsdom 24 (returns null). It surfaces deep
inside the minified webpack bundle's jQuery click handler chain, so
pinpointing the exact attribute requires either a debug bundle build or
a bisect of jsdom intermediate versions. Not blocking — Phase 3.6
modernizes this test directly (red/green) and the new test will not
load the bundle at all.

Decision
========
Phase 2 (deps lift) proceeds with USE_BENV_SHIM=1 as the CI default for
5 of 6 tests. profileeditor stays on real benv (USE_BENV_SHIM=0 in its
test invocation) until Phase 3.6 retires it. This is documented as the
gating condition for Phase 4 ("drop benv") — benv must remain installed
until profileeditor's modernized test exists.
